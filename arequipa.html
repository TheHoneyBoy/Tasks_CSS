About PhawAI

At PhawAI 2025, the Onsite phase represents the space where the community meets in person to share knowledge, experiences, and opportunities in artificial intelligence. During these two days, experts and students participate in workshops, presentations, and panels on key topics such as responsible AI, model interpretability, and study abroad. It also includes interactive sessions and networking opportunities, strengthening collaboration in the field of AI in Peru.
